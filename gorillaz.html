A few facts about my favorite band, Gorillaz.

Gorillaz are an English virtual band formed in 1998 by musician Damon Albarn and artist Jamie Hewlett, from London.

The band mainly consists of 4 fictional members

  • 2-D
  • Murdoc Niccals
  • Noodle
  • Russel Hobbs
